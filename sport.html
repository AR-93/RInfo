Real Madrid boycotts 2024 Ballon d'Or after Rodri's win over Vinicius Jr.

The 2024 Ballon d'Or choice of Manchester City's Rodri over Real Madrid's Vinicius Junior has stirred controversy. Despite Vinicius’s 26 goals and 11 assists, Real Madrid seems discontent, viewing the decision as politically influenced. Many argue Rodri's contributions fall short of Vinicius's on-field impact, raising questions about the award's criteria and impartiality.

Madrid Shares Footage to Identify Racists from 'El Clasico

La Liga has submitted new evidence to the Madrid Provincial Information Brigade regarding racist insults during 'El Clasico' at the Santiago Bernabéu. Real Madrid is assisting in identifying those responsible for the abuse directed at Barcelona players. The incidents, recorded by spectators, are under investigation by the Hate Crimes and Discrimination Unit, as La Liga seeks stronger measures against violence in football.

TotalEnergies AFCON 2025 in Morocco: Key Information You Need to Know

Morocco is set to host the 2025 TotalEnergies Africa Cup of Nations (AFCON), marking its second time since 1988. The tournament will feature 24 teams, with qualifiers starting in September 2024. Key venues include Mohammed V Stadium and Moulay Abdellah Stadium. The event runs from December 21, 2025, to January 18, 2026, showcasing Morocco's growing football reputation and infrastructure.
